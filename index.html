About Me

Hello! I'm Gisèle Abizeye Haguma, and this is my personal space to share a bit about myself, my passions, and my journey. I'm an artistic soul with a professional outlook, always eager to learn and create.

My interests span from the captivating worlds of movies and TV shows to the thrill of entertainment events. I'm a foodie at heart, always on the lookout for the next delicious meal, and I love to travel and experience new cultures.

Here, you'll find reflections on my experiences, peeks into my creative projects, and stories from my adventures. My goal is to connect with family and friends, and hopefully inspire a little joy and creativity along the way.
